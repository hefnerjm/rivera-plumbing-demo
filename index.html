Our Story

Where tradition rises

Abigail grew up watching her grandmother roll dough on Sunday mornings — the kitchen thick with cinnamon and the sound of family gathering. What started as ritual became calling.

Today, Abigail's Bakery carries that warmth into every batch. Whether it's a tray of conchas kissed with colored sugar or a rustic sourdough with a crackling crust, every item is handmade, in small batches, with real ingredients.

We bake out of love for San Antonio and the people in it.
